About Us

The Molecular Design Group at Dublin City University is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of chemical research through innovative computational and experimental techniques. Our interdisciplinary team focuses on the design, synthesis, and characterization of novel molecules with potential applications in fields such as catalysis, materials science, and drug discovery. We are committed to excellence in research, fostering a collaborative environment, and training the next generation of scientific leaders.
